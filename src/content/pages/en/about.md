# What LIFO is

**Local Innovation Forum (LIFO)** is a B2B conference that closes a specific gap:

> **Regions have projects. The private sector has capital. But they only meet by chance — or not at all.**

LIFO closes this gap through a **curated format** — no filler panels, no "another networking event". The goal is for participants to leave not with a brochure, but with a **concrete contact**, a **blueprint**, or a **proven financing model**.

## Who it's for

- **Mayors and municipal leaders** seeking funding for projects beyond the state budget
- **Investors, developers, banks** needing access to investment-ready sites and decision-makers
- **Ministries and public agencies** that shape the legislative framework
- **Consultants and technology firms** delivering tools and know-how

## What makes LIFO different

**LIFO does not solve "policy". It solves "delivery".**

| Other conferences            | LIFO                                                       |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Panel discussions and visions | 20-minute matchmaking sessions based on real criteria      |
| "Inspirational" talks         | Concrete case studies from working projects                |
| Generic networking            | Curated attendee list with verified potential              |
| Politically tinted formats    | Neutral expert space                                       |
| Brochure of recommendations   | Blueprint with risk checklist and next steps               |
