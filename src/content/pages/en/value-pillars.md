# What you'll solve here

Concrete value depending on who you are.

## For municipalities

- **Matchmaking** — curated 20-minute meetings with investors based on concrete criteria. Not generic pitching.
- **Readiness workshop** — due diligence, financing models (PPP, EPC, EIB) at a practical level. After LIFO you'll be able to present your project in the language investors understand.
- **Blueprint** — concrete playbooks from working case studies in Czechia, Poland, Austria. No theory.
- **Early access to Investment Map Slovakia** *(advance access for attendees)*

## For investors, developers, banks

- **Curated dealflow** — at least 150 municipal representatives with verified investment potential. Not random lobby encounters.
- **Investment Map Slovakia** — site passportization including:
  - Area, ownership, utility infrastructure
  - Zoning plan and environmental limits (EIA)
  - Demographic profile and labor supply in the 30-minute commuter zone
  - **IRL score** (Investment Readiness Level) — A = ready for negotiation, B = processes in progress, C = identified potential
- **Sector exclusivity** in partnership tiers — no direct competitor in the same tier

## For partners and sponsors

- **Main stage** with exposure to decision-makers in the state and municipalities
- **1:1 meetings** with TOP 10 mayors for the General Partner tier
- **Brand association** with the first seriously curated format of its kind in Slovakia
- **Post-forum support**:
  - Final Investment Map delivered within 7 days after the event
  - Detailed matchmaking report with prioritized next steps
  - Facilitation of the first official negotiation between investor and municipality *(included for General and Main Partners)*

## For speakers

- **An audience that decides** — mayors, ministries, investment-firm CEOs, EIB representatives, top developers
- **A format that is not about self-promotion**, but about real delivery of topics
- Opportunity to become a **thematic expert lead** for a program block
