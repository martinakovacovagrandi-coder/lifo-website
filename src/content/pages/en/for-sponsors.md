# For sponsors and partners

**Become a partner of the first seriously curated format where private capital meets investment-ready municipalities.**

## Why partner with LIFO

LIFO does not sell sponsorship packages — it sells **access**. Specifically:

- **Access to decision-makers** — mayors, ministries, investment-firm CEOs, EIB, top developers
- **Access to dealflow** — a curated list of municipalities with verified investment potential
- **Brand association** with a format that positions itself as *delivery, not policy*

## ROI rationale

Identifying and validating a suitable site today often takes **9 to 14 months** and generates significant costs in internal team allocation and external due diligence.

LIFO dramatically **shortens** this process by concentrating ready-to-invest municipalities in one place. Partnership (from € XXX) represents a **fraction** of these internal costs while delivering higher value through guided matchmaking and direct contact with decision-makers.
