## 2. What data we process

### 2.1 When browsing the website

- **No cookies are set.**
- **No analytics tools** (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel and similar) are used.
- Our hosting provider (Netlify) may log your IP address in standard server logs for security and abuse-prevention purposes. These logs are auto-deleted after 30 days.
- Web fonts (Inter) are **hosted directly on our server** — no data is transferred to third parties when loading them.

### 2.2 When submitting the contact form

We collect the following personal data that you voluntarily provide:

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Organization** *(optional)*
- **Message content**
- **Segment** *(partner / speaker / municipality / media / other)*

## 4. Retention period

- **Contact form submissions**: maximum **24 months** after the LIFO 2027 conference (i.e., until February 2029), unless an ongoing contractual or communication relationship exists with you.
- **Upon deletion request**: we delete your data without undue delay (max 30 days).

## 5. Recipients of your data

For website operation, we use the following processors:

- **Netlify, Inc.** (USA / EU regions) — website hosting and contact form processing. Netlify complies with the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.
- **Brevo (Sendinblue)** (France, EU) — *to be added if we start sending a newsletter*

We **do not sell** your data to any third parties. We do not use it for third-party marketing.

## 6. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right:

- **To access** your personal data (request a copy)
- **To rectification** of inaccurate data
- **To erasure** ("right to be forgotten")
- **To restriction** of processing
- **To data portability**
- **To object** to processing based on legitimate interest
- **To withdraw consent** at any time
- **To lodge a complaint** with the Slovak Data Protection Authority ([dataprotection.gov.sk](https://dataprotection.gov.sk))

### How to exercise your rights

Email us at our contact address *(see section 1)*. We will respond within **30 days**.

## 7. Security

- The website communicates exclusively via **HTTPS** (TLS encryption).
- Form data is stored in a secure Netlify Forms inbox, accessible only to authorized LIFO team members.
- We regularly review access and log any suspicious activity.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. For material changes, we will notify you via email (if provided) or by updating this page. The current version is always available at **lifo.urbanlama.eu/en/privacy**.
