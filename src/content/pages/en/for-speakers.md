# For speakers

**We're looking for experts who can deliver concrete know-how — not self-promotion.**

## Who we're looking for

LIFO is a **working conference**, not an inspirational event. We're looking for speakers with **real experience** in any of the following:

- **PPP, EPC and alternative financing models** for public projects
- **Investment readiness of municipalities** — site passportization, zoning, due diligence
- **Real case studies** of successful project implementation (SK / CZ / PL / AT / DE)
- **Energy communities, smart city, decarbonization** at a practical level
- **Regional transformation** around giga-investments (Volvo, JLR and similar)
- **Public procurement and legislation** at a practical level

## Format

LIFO **does not use traditional panel discussions**. Instead:

- **Case Breakdown** *(20–30 min)* — deconstruction of one real project including mistakes, blockers and lessons learned
- **Working panel** *(40 min)* — moderated structured dialogue of max. 3 people with commitment to concrete actionable takeaways
- **Workshop** *(60–90 min)* — for municipalities, hands-on format with working materials

## What we offer speakers

- **An audience that decides** — mayors, ministries, investment-firm CEOs, top developers
- **Professional production** — AV, simultaneous interpretation for selected panels, quality recording
- **Branding** in the LIFO communication campaign
- **Networking** with other speakers and partners outside the program
- **Opportunity to become a thematic expert lead** for a program block in future editions

## What we don't offer

- **Speaking fee** *(with the exception of invited international keynote speakers — handled individually)*
- **Commercial pitches** — LIFO reserves the right to curate content to prevent the format from turning into a sales pitch

## How to apply

Send us:

1. **Short bio** *(max 5 sentences)*
2. **Proposed talk topic** with one sentence on what the audience will take away
3. **Concrete case study or real experience** the talk is built on
4. **LinkedIn or website** for context

We'll get back to you within **5 business days**.
