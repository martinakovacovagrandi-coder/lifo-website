# Why 2027

**Fiscal consolidation pressure and the giga-investment wave converge in a single year.**

## Municipalities: the toughest fiscal year in decades

Slovakia enters the toughest budgetary year for municipalities in recent decades. Three converging waves of public finance consolidation will strip nearly **€500 million** from local budgets. Cities will have to find funding **outside the state budget**:

- PPP (Public-Private Partnership) models
- EPC (Energy Performance Contracting)
- Energy communities
- Private capital and EIB lending

## Industry: the largest investment wave since 2004

At the same time, the largest wave of industrial and infrastructure investment is unfolding:

- **Volvo Košice** — serial EV production launched, **12,000+ jobs** including suppliers, regional GDP impact +1.3 %
- **PPP overhaul of 575 bridges** — the government launched a massive concession model to repair primary-route bridges
- **Rental housing** near industrial parks has become a systemic challenge for municipalities

## Triangulation of interests

LIFO connects three layers that today work in isolation:

1. **The state** provides legislation and framework
2. **Industry and developers** provide capital and the need to deliver
3. **Consultants and technology** provide tools and efficiency

In 2027, those with the right contacts and ready projects will **catch the wave**. Those without will pay through expensive consolidation loans — or missed opportunities.
